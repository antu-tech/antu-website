職場推薦信撰寫指南

解析執行力、理論實務結合與專業韌性

職場推薦信的核心價值

對於有工作經驗的申請者或申請專業碩士（如 MBA, MPH, MSCS-Professional）的人來說，職場推薦信是展現實踐能力與團隊貢獻的最佳機會。

  • 執行力 (Execution)：你是否能將模糊的商業目標轉化為具體的產出？
  • 問題解決 (Conflict & Crisis)：面對死線、預算壓力或衝突時，你的應對方式為何？
  • 成長潛力 (Scale & Growth)：你的貢獻是否具備可擴展性？

本範本以「資深軟體工程實習生」在敏捷開發團隊中的表現為背景，展示如何透過主管視角，精準捕捉職場中的技術成長與領袖特質。

第一段：背景與職責界定

"As the candidate's direct manager during their tenure at our cloud infrastructure team, I observed a professional maturity that far exceeded their peer group. Tasked with optimizing our automated billing pipeline, they demonstrated an immediate grasp of our complex system architecture, moving from orientation to active contribution within their first week."

顧問點評

強調「一週內即有產出」展現了極強的適應力（Adaptability）。在快節奏的職場中，降低教育成本是雇主最看重的特質之一。

第二段：具體商業價值產出

"Their most notable contribution involved refactoring our legacy data ingestion module. By implementing an asynchronous queueing strategy, they successfully reduced latency by 45%, a metric that translated into direct operational savings for our enterprise clients. This was not simply a technical task; it required a strategic understanding of how code affects the bottom line."

顧問點評

使用具體數據「45%」增強了可信度。將技術動作與商業成果（Operational savings）連結，證明申請者具備 Big Picture 視野。

第三段：團隊影響力與韌性

"Beyond their technical prowess, the candidate was a stabilising force during our high-pressure v2.0 launch phase. They proactively mentored junior interns and consistently volunteered for on-call duties during critical migration windows. Their professional resilience and willingness to support the team's broader objectives made them an indispensable asset to our departmental culture."

顧問點評

「穩定力量」（Stabilising force）與「指導後進」（Mentoring）描述了其軟實力。在高壓環境下的表現，是名校招生官判斷申請者 EQ 的重要指標。

第四段：職涯潛力與最終推薦

"The candidate possesses a rare combination of technical rigor and strategic foresight. I have no doubt that they will emerge as a leader in the global technology landscape. I offer my strongest endorsement for their graduate studies and look forward to their future professional milestones."

顧問點評

結尾展現了主管對其未來「地標性成就」（Milestones）的期待。這種長線的肯定，能賦予申請者高度的公信力。
